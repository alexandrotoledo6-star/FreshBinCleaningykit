FAQ

Questions answered.

Everything you need to know before booking your first clean.

Our truck arrives on your collection morning (while your bins are already at the curb). We pressure-wash the inside and outside with hot water and eco-certified cleaning agents, apply a deodorizer, and return the bins spotless. You get a before/after photo via text — the whole process takes about 5 minutes per bin.
Not at all! Just leave your bins at the curb on your normal collection day. We'll handle everything and let you know once it's done. If you have a gate or special access, just leave a note in the booking form.
We currently serve Toronto and the greater GTA including North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, and Brampton. Not sure if you're in our zone? Enter your postal code in the booking form and we'll confirm within 2 hours.
The Pro Monthly plan is your regular recurring clean — great for keeping bins fresh year-round. Deep Sanitization is an intensive one-time treatment using hospital-grade chemicals and 180°F hot water, designed for severely soiled bins, heavy mold, maggot infestations, or post-summer deep cleans. Many customers do a Deep Clean first, then switch to Pro Monthly to maintain it.
Yes — all our products are eco-certified, biodegradable, and non-toxic once dried. We use hospital-grade sanitizers only on Deep Sanitization cleans, and even those are fully safe once the bin has air-dried (about 20 minutes). No harsh residue is left behind.
Super easy. Just text or email us anytime — no portals, no forms. We're a small team and we'll handle it personally, same day. You won't be charged for any future cleanings after cancellation, and there are no cancellation fees whatsoever.
Pro Monthly and Deep Sanitization plans include a free re-clean guarantee. If you're not satisfied, just let us know within 48 hours and we'll come back out at no charge. For One-Time cleans, we'll always work with you to make it right — our reputation depends on it.
